WHY E-WASTE MATTERS

Proper disposal isn't just a minor issue — it has immense logical and environmental consequences.

🖥️
62M Tons

Global electronic waste generated in 2022. This is projected to climb to 82M tons by 2030.

💎
$91B USD Lost

Valuable materials like copper, iron, and gold thrown out annually. Proper circuit salvage recovers this resource stream.

♻️
22.3% Recycled

Only a tiny fraction of total international e-waste is formally verified, processed, and reintroduced into circular supply chains.

⚠️
85% Toxic Landfills

Most materials release mercury, lead, and flame retardants into the water supply line if disposed of in municipal regular bins.

Source: World Health Organization & International Telecommunication Union (WHO / ITU). (2024).

Circular Economic Guidance

💡 The 50% Economic Logic

If estimated repairs breach 50% of buying a modern equal product, replacing generally provides better material reliability, unless the device carries custom components.

🛠️ Parts Sourcing Impact

Extremely rare logic panels increase delivery overhead. Opting for third-party certified repair shops often manages total repair costs below corporate quotes.

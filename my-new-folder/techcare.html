📐 Screen Alignment

If visual screens experience flickering lines or ghost touches, do not immediately buy modules. Clear connection cables of dust indices:

  1. Dismount surrounding rubber bezels.
  2. Expose primary ribbon connectors.
  3. Saponify connector pads with isopropylic elements gently.

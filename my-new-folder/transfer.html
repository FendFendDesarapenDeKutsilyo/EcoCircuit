Environmental Precedence Rules

📦 Component Isolation

Always isolate and strip lithium-ion batteries from aluminum housings before disposal. Incinerating battery units creates extreme local toxicity risks.

🏭 Metal Refining

Delivering boards to specialized refiners ensures that heavy chemical residuals elements (e.g. lead, mercury) are handled in vacuum-tight smelting rooms.
